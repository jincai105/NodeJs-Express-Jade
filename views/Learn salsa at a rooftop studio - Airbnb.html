About your host, Damian Alejandro
I am the owner and founder of Salsa Express. I've been working in this business for more than 10 years—six years with my own studio. I have taught salsa in France and the USA. I realized along the way that teaching salsa moves me beyond measure, and it's great to be able to export one of our national symbols. Nowadays I instruct my teachers on how to teach, which is a great experience.
What we’ll do
A vintage car will pick you up and bring you to the dance studio. Depending on where you're being picked up, you may get bonus views of the city along the way. We will welcome you at the studio and take you to the terrace, where the school is located. There you will meet your teacher, who will show you the basic salsa steps. Where we’ll be
You will visit a private Cuban family business located on our terrace. You will also get to see part of the city on the way to and from the studio. It is important for you to plug into our reality by dancing and enjoying life the way we do.
